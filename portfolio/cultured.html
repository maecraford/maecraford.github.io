Cultured

Cultured Stickers was a company founded the summer of 2016.

When Molly Wu and I met at MICA Pre-College we instantly connected over our shared love of stickers. By the time the summer was over we had an amazing friendship, and the start to a great company.

We got orders from around the world and we got orders of all different sizes.

Below is a collection of a few stickers we created and sold.

Over the next two years we sold about 60 different stickers.

All of the designs were created and manufactured by us.

We sold them through Etsy and found a following of people who loved stickers just as much
as we do.

Cultured was so loved we were the number 1 search that came up when you looked for
stickers on Etsy.

When the time for college came Molly and I decided to scale back the company to only sell a selection of ten stickers. While we were still receiving orders it became secondary to
our school work.

By the time sophomore year started, neither Molly or I had the time or space to continue creating and selling the stickers.

We chose to close up shop in October of 2018, two years after we began.

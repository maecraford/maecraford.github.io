Cultured

Cultured was a design studio which topped the sticker charts on Etsy from 2016 to 2018.

Founded by myself, and fellow creative Molly Wu, through a mutual love of accessible art and pop culture iconography. Cultured sold more than 60 different sticker designs to customers world wide.

All merchandise was designed, manufactured, and distributed in house. Custom design orders and bulk orders for resale in retail storefronts were common occurrences and led to further brand development.

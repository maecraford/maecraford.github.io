escape

escape is an archival website of escapism

For my senior thesis project at Parsons I chose to focus on studying different ways people escape and I found that in comparing a plethora of different types of escapsim you begin to understand why people are so
drawn to it.

I was initally intrigued by the spike in usage in three key terms in the past centery.

This lead me to my first iteration of this project which was a bible.

I was intrigued by the crossovers in fandoms and cults, which led me to dive deeper into comparisons in ways people find their way to escape.

My final thesis project began with the exploration of the word escapsim and how it is used in our everyday lives.

Escapism is the tendency to seek distraction and relief from unpleasant realities, especially by seeking entertainment or engaging in fantasy. This definition is very broad, there are many different forms of escapism which people subscribe to.

In order to cover lots of territory in my archival website I decided to break it into
four catagories.

I also added several other pages to add more information for any viewer of the archive. Including a quiz for people to contribute how they escape and compare their results with others.

The website had lot of fun interactive features and moving buttons. I was inspired by early web design because it was known as an era of exploration and the internet became a haven for those feeling marginilized.

Jello

During my Junior year I began to focus on branding.

One of the projects I worked on involved choosing a current brand that is failing and rebranding it.

I chose the family favorite JELL-O.

My Jello rebrand proposal

I looked into JELL-Oâ€™s current branding and spent time seeing where they were failing and how I could change that. I found the company was spending too much time marketing to children.

As sugary treats from the past I felt that they had a better market with people between the ages of 18-35. I decided to rebrand their packaging with a more millennial feeling to it.

I repackaged three of the most popular JELL-O flavours.
I also created a fictional line of JELL-O Mystery, to add flavor back into the brand.

Each package features a creation tip to spark the imagination of a consumer.

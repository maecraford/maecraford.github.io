Want to know more about Mae?

I am a graphic designer who knows her way around a website, while staying true to her life long love of branding. Various talents included in my tool belt range from hand painting posters, to shooting short films, the occasional sticker making, and even good oldfashion printmaking.

As a recent graduate of Parsons School of Design I have been freelancing in different intersections of design to gain perspective and experience. My work has lead me to clients in industries ranging from entertainment to biotechnology.

Along with design I spend my days jamming out to tunes I love, re watching movies that made me, and learning new things. For more on me, you can read the maezine.

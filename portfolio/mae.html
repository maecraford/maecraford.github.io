Mae

Hello dear visitor,

My name is Mae Craford and I am a creative who loves learning new things.Since a child I have been introguied by two things, the art of branding and designs ability to enhance a story.

As a recent graduate of Parsons School of Design I have been putting my BFA in communication design to use creating tailored websites and brand identities for fellow creative spirits. I have a passion for enabling clients dreams to become a tangible product. My experiences have given me skills in leadership, collaboration, brand development, and website creation. In addition to technical skills such as Adobe Creative Suite, Figma, HTML/CSS, typography, color theory, design standards, and editorial composition.

In the past I have worked with non-profits, musicians, and small business's in the entertainment and biotech industries. My talents also include being an Art Teacher to clients whose ages range from 1 to 90. I've been known to bake the perfect loaf of sourdough, rock out to music made 30 years before I was born, and even instruct the occasional wine & paint for certain crowds.


Gathered here are two projects I created during the past few years which help to tell my story.

Maezine

The Maezine is a small book filled with fun facts which intrigue me and relate to myself.

Platonic Solids

Platonic Solids is a collection of faux reference letters I had my friends write for me combined with an image I drew of each of them. Their written views of me, and my artistic views of them.

Some letters are a little more humorous than others.

I hope these items assisted in painting a better story of myself. If you wish to explore my work further, click on any of the other links on my website home page.

Mae

Hello dear visitor,

My name is Mae Craford and I am a creative who loves learning new things.
I recently graduated from Parsons School for Design with a BFA in Communication Design. During my time there I found interest in branding and became fascinated with enhancing a story
through design.

In the spirit of that statement, gathered here are several projects I have completed which help to tell my story.

Maezine

The Maezine is a small book filled with fun facts which intrigue me and relate to myself.

One of my earlier projects about myself is also a zine, however this time the text is about me by someone else.

Platonic Solids

Platonic Solids is a collection of faux reference letters I had my friends write for me combined with an image I drew of each of them. Their written views of me, and my artistic views of them.

Some letters are a little more humorous than others.

I hope these items assisted in painting a better story of myself. If you wish to explore my work further, click on any of the other links on my website home page.

salvation

Salvation is a spin on a childrens encyclopedia

The twist is that all the terms are related to finding salvation. I comprised the list after a semester of research into the rise of salvation in America since the 1960's. It was another project I finished during my thesis, as an informational added piece.

For each letter I chose a word which has resonence in peoples journey for salvation. I accompanied each entry with a definiton, an image or video, and links for further research.

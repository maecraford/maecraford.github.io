Below is a scrolling list of video based projects I have worked on.
Each project features the title of the work, a descirption, and the video.

The Quitter

This experimental video is another iteration of my exploration into the poem The Quitter by Robert Service. I wanted to capture a more modern take of the words displayed in the lines. The quick paced screen recorded video gives a feeling of constant motion and being lost in time.

waves

For my final project freshman year I chose to make a short docuseries about how people are using music today. The six episode series is a compilation of interviews I did with people in Washington Square Park in New York City.

Due to the pandemic, during my senior year at Parsons I moved to Park City, Utah to ski with my father for three months. At the same time I was taking a screen studies course. One of our assignments was to film our surroundings for a day and compile it into a short video.

I created this 5 minute video as a presentation of the material I had been studying during my independent study into material culture. This video weaves theory from derrida and barthes with the cinematic universe of spiderman. In studying deconstruction and becoming I found that displaying the things I had learned in pop culture refrences made it more palatable for people.

i don't know

I Don't Know was created based on the idea of bouncing objects. I had trouble coming up with the idea for this piece in terms of what I wanted it to say. I am very indecisive. To fix my creative block I asked two of my friends what I should create my piece around. One said shrimp and the other said chicken. The first thing that came to mind was a top ramen package. The rest is best shown and not described.

dialogue

During my Sophmore year at Parsons I was assigned a project in which we were instructed to create a website built around a piece of found diologue. The piece I decided to create was based around the opening scene from Monty Python and the Holy Grail. In the scene King Arthur debates with a soldier about the migration of coconuts. The piece is simple and hilarious. When creating the website we had certain perameter. We had to use only a singluar typeface and we could only use text to emphasise the dictation and emotions in the scene.
